BloodConnect

Nepal's largest Open-Source Blood Bank Management System

BloodConnect is a community-driven platform designed to streamline blood donation and management in Nepal. Our mission is to connect donors, recipients, and healthcare providers to ensure that every drop of blood counts.

We believe in the power of open-source technology to create a transparent, efficient, and accessible blood management system for all.

Our Mission

To build a reliable, transparent, and community-driven blood donation system that saves lives by connecting donors and recipients across Nepal.

How It Works

  1. Register as a donor or recipient on our platform.
  2. Search for available blood types in your area or post a blood request.
  3. Connect directly with donors or blood banks.
  4. Coordinate safe and timely blood donations.

Get Involved

Join us in making a difference. You can help by donating blood, volunteering, spreading awareness, or contributing to our open-source codebase on GitHub.
