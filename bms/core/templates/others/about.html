BloodConnect

Nepal's largest Open-Source Blood Bank Management System

BloodConnect is a community-driven platform designed to streamline blood donation and management in Nepal. Our mission is to connect donors, recipients, and healthcare providers to ensure that every drop of blood counts.

We believe in the power of open-source technology to create a transparent, efficient, and accessible blood management system for all.
