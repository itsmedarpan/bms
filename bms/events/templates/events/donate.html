Become a Donor

Why Donate Blood?

  • Every donation can save up to three lives.
  • Blood cannot be manufactured; it can only come from donors like you.
  • It’s a simple, safe, and quick process.

Who Can Donate?

  • Age 18-65, in good health
  • Weigh at least 50kg
  • No recent illness or major surgery
  • Not pregnant or breastfeeding

Register as a Donor

How Donation Works

  1. Register as a donor using the form above.
  2. We’ll contact you to schedule a convenient time.
  3. Donate at our center or a local event.
  4. Receive updates when your blood helps someone!
